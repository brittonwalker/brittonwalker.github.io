Techonology sensitive to
the practice of meditation.

GENERATIVE SOUND MEDITATIONS

Each session, Non generates a unique audio composition note by note based on your preference, environment, time of day, and motion.

ADAPTIVE VISUALS

Based on your current location and position relative to the sun, Non’s user interface adapts with a unique, colorful visualization reflective of the mood of that particular time of day.

CALMING OR FOCUSING

Each sound meditation adjusts to your desired mental state, whether you’d like to relax at the end of the day or remain focused at work.

A SINGLE GESTURE

Swipe, press, release, and begin. A single gestural sequence initiates an immediate, seamless experience. Every time.
*iPhone 6s+ only

TACTILE & CONNECTIVE

Nothing stands between you and your meditation. Utilizing haptic feedback, each gesture is fully integrated with your experience. Sound sequences sync with any movement detected by your phone’s accelerometer.

UNIQUE AUDIO PROCESSING

Morning, afternoon, or night, in the comfort of your home or on a crowded train, Non is mastered to sound beautiful on your mobile device, headphones, or external speakers.

TIMER MODE

Non can be used as a timer for those practicing silent meditation. Clarifying bells mark the beginning and end of each session.

Available for iPhone
